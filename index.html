About

I am a custom software and product systems developer with a background in commercial configurators, custom CPQ systems, technical implementation, and business-to-software translation.

I took a career break from 2022 to 2025, and I have been rebuilding my technical proficiency since — the projects and methods on this site are that work.

From 2011 to 2022, I built and supported commercial configurator and custom CPQ systems for configurable physical products including sheds, playsets, and fencing. The shed configurator began in 2012; in 2015–2016 I acquired the customization business and ran it independently as 3D Customizer, in production through 2022. That work included product rules, live pricing, quote and order paths, customer documents, staff tools, and sales operations.

My flagship commercial project was a production Unity configurator and custom CPQ system that powered custom shed sales from configuration through quote, order, rent-to-own, and staff reference materials. The system supported thousands of quote, order, and rent-to-own records tied to millions of dollars in shed sales activity.

Before operating 3D Customizer independently, I worked in a small agency environment where I regularly served as the technical-to-business bridge across 40 to 60 client accounts, from small businesses to multimillion-dollar companies. I joined owner and client meetings, clarified what was technically feasible, and turned business goals, sales ideas, and product requirements into deliverable software, configurator, web, AR, and media projects.

My hands-on background in construction, sales, warehouse management, and B2B marketing gives me practical context for building software around real products, real customers, and real business operations.
